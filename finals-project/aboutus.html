A HISTORY

The Wangsheng Funeral Parlor was originally founded during or after the Archon War, when the fallen gods manifested demons that prompted Rex Lapis to send the Yaksha to protect Liyue. Since then, it has existed for 77 generations. These people pioneered a new path and eventually founded the parlor. They and their successors would tirelessly maintain the balance between life and death.

HU TAO

The 77th Funeral Director, and a talented poet. Possessing a Pyro Vision, she has an upbeat and whimsical personality. When the business gets going, she attends to the rites with solemnity and respect.

ZHONGLI

The funeral consultant of the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor. He is an elegant gentleman with an abundance of knowledge. A master of courtesy and rules, he is well-respected by the people. Apparently, he is the vessel of the Geo Archon, Morax.

ANA ALIMURUNG

The head undertaker and technical manager of the parlor. Despite her rigid diligence, when long breaks come, she becomes a major procrastinator and deals with stuff right after the break. Mr. Zhongli would often scold her for this drawback and Ferrylady would make sure that she gets her duties done before she slacks off.
