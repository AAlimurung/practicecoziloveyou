Flower of Life

Flower of Life, or simply "the Flower," is an Artifact Piece in Genshin Impact.
It increases a character's HP stat by adding flat +HP and is one of the two
Artifact Pieces to have a set main stat (along with the Plume of Death).
Artifact Pieces can appear in all rarities (1 to 5 stars).

It is considered to be one of the easiest pieces to get "good stats" on because of its set main stat:
the variance is therefore strict to the substats. Note that Artifacts cannot share the same main stat
as substats, so Flowers of Life cannot have the flat +HP substat. It is possible, however, to have
+HP% in a Flower since it is considered a different type of substat.
