Plume of Death

Plume of Death, or simply "the Feather," is an Artifact Piece in Genshin Impact.
It increases a character's ATK stat by adding flat +ATK and is one of the two
Artifact Pieces to have a set main stat (along with the Flower of Life).
Artifact Pieces can appear in all rarities (1 to 5 stars).

It is considered to be one of the easiest pieces to get "good stats" on because of its set main stat:
the variance is therefore strict to the substats. Note that Artifacts cannot share the same main stat
as substats, so Plumes of Death cannot have the flat +ATK substat. It is possible, however, to have
+ATK% in a Plume since it is considered a different type of substat.
