The Primordials


These are the first gods perceived by the Greeks,
starting with Chaos.


  • Chaos | the void
  • Gaia | goddess of the earth
  • Ouranos | god of the sky
  • Erebos | god of darkness
  • Nyx | goddess of the night
  • Hemera | goddess of day, daughter of Nyx
  • Aether | god of light, son of Nyx
  • Tartarus | god of pit
  • Pontus | god of the first oceans

The Titans


Most of these titans are sons and daughters of the primordial gods Gaia and Ouranus.
However there are other titans that aren't children of the aforementioned gods.


    The Titan children

  • Krios | titan of the south
  • Koios | titan of the north
  • Hyperion | titan of the east
  • Iapetus | titan of the west
  • Oceanus | titan of the seas
  • Kronos | titan of time
  • Tethys | titaness of fresh waters
  • Theia | titaness of clear sights
  • Themis | titaness of natural law
  • Phoebe | titaness of prophecies
  • Mnemosyne | titaness of memory
  • Rhea | the mother goddess

    • The children

  • Helios | titan of the sun
    son of Hyperion and Theia
  • Selene | titaness of the moon
    daughter of Hyperion and Theia
  • Astraeus | titan of the dusk
    son of Krios and Eurybia
  • Eos | titaness of the dawn
    daughter of Hyperion and Theia
  • Prometheus | titan of forethought and fire
    son of Iapetus and Klymene
  • Atlas | Titan, son of Iapetus and Klymene

The Olympians


Six of these gods are children of Kronos and Rhea. The other gods are children of Hera and Zeus, and the other lovers of Zeus.
And for the love of life, some of them have many titles, but I'll choose the most relevant of them all.


    The six original

  • Hestia | goddess of the hearth
  • Demeter | goddess of harvest and agriculture
  • Hera | goddess of marriage and family
  • Hades | god of the dead and the underworld
  • Poseidon | god of the seas and earthquakes
  • Zeus | god of thunder and the sky

    • The rest

  • Athena | goddess of wisdom and warcraft
    daughter of Zeus and Metis
  • Aphrodite | goddess of love and beauty
  • Artemis | goddess of the moon, the maidens and the hunt
    daughter of Zeus and Leto
  • Apollo | god of the sun, music, healing, and prophecies
    son of Zeus and Leto
  • Hermes | messenger god of thieves, and boundaries
    son of Zeus and Maia
  • Dionysus | god of wine and insanity
    son of Zeus and Semele
  • Ares | god of war
    son of Hera and Zeus
  • Hephaestus | god of fire and blacksmiths
    son of Hera
